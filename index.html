About Zazi

We are a Durban based art and graphic design company that works with highly skilled and qualified visual artists and graphic designers. We work with local artists on conceptualising and executions of our projects. Our project are led by the award winning visual artist and graphic designer Zazi Nxumalo.

We specialise on murals and we have done quite a number of projects around Durban. We have worked with EThekwini municipality on a City-Wide Mural Project that covers all the areas of Durban form Tongaat area to Umkomaas. We have worked with local businesses and international brands. We believe in job creation as we employ highly skilled artists to help them make a living out of their skills and creativity.
